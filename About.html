What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the protection of computer systems and networks from information disclosure, theft of or damage to their hardware, software, or electronic data, as well as from the disruption or misdirection of the services they provide.

Why is it Important?

It protects all categories of data from theft and damage.

What are the biggest threats to online safety?

Phishing Attacks, Malware Attacks, Ransomware, Weak Passwords, Insider Threats

What are strategies that can be used to combat cyberthreats?

Developing cyber security policies. Implementing security awareness training. Installing spam filters and anti-malware software.
